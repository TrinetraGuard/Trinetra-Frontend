This Privacy Policy describes how **Nisarga Lokhande** ("we," "us," or "developer") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the **Trinetra** mobile application ("App") on Android devices. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Introduction

Trinetra is a safety and pilgrimage management application designed to improve safety, crowd management, emergency response, and communication during pilgrimage events. The App is intended for pilgrims, volunteers, administrators, and the general public. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### 2.1 Personal Information

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number (when provided by you)
- Account credentials and profile information associated with your role (e.g., volunteer, administrator, user)

### 2.2 Device Information

- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system and version
- App version
- Unique device identifiers (where necessary for the operation of the App)

### 2.3 Location Data

If you enable location services, we may collect and process your device's location data (e.g., latitude and longitude) to provide:

- Navigation and safety features
- Emergency response and SOS functionality
- Location-based alerts and updates
- Lost and found reporting with place-of-incident information

You can disable location access in your device settings; some features may not work without it.

### 2.4 Camera Access

With your permission, we may access your device's camera to allow you to report incidents or upload photos (e.g., for lost person reports). Photos are used only for the purposes you authorize within the App.

### 2.5 Usage Data and Analytics

We may collect information about how you use the App, such as feature usage, session duration, and general interaction data, to improve performance and user experience.

### 2.6 Push Notification Tokens

To send you push notifications (e.g., emergency alerts, announcements, or important updates), we may collect and use notification tokens provided by your device or by Firebase Cloud Messaging.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect and process information in accordance with their own privacy policies:

### 3.1 Google Firebase

- **Firebase Authentication** — for user sign-in and account management
- **Cloud Firestore** — for storing app data (e.g., user profiles, reports, alerts)
- **Firebase Cloud Storage** — for storing files such as images (e.g., lost person photos)

Google's privacy policy applies to Firebase services: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### 3.2 Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

We use FCM to deliver push notifications. FCM may collect device and usage information as described in Google's privacy policy.

### 3.3 Google AdMob

We may display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and device information for ad delivery and measurement. For more information, see the Advertising section below and Google's advertising privacy policy.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers to understand how they handle your information.
