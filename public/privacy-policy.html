Privacy Policy

Trinetra — Android Mobile Application

Effective Date: 27 Feb 2026

Last Updated: 15 March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nisarga Lokhande (“we,” “us,” or “developer”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Trinetra mobile application (“App”) on Android devices. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Availability of this policy: This Privacy Policy is available within the Trinetra app and at a publicly accessible URL for the duration of the app's availability on Google Play. The same policy applies to the app listed as Trinetra (or trinetra-app-frontend) on the Google Play Store. Our data practices are also summarized in the Data safety section on the app's Google Play Store listing.

1. Introduction

Trinetra is a safety and pilgrimage management application designed to improve safety, crowd management, emergency response, and communication during pilgrimage events. The App is intended for pilgrims, volunteers, administrators, and the general public. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information. Where required by Google Play or applicable law, we distinguish between data that is required for the app to function (e.g., account info for sign-in, location for safety features when you use them) and data that is optional (e.g., analytics, personalized ads). You can control optional collection via device settings and in-app choices where offered.

We may collect the following types of information:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (when provided by you)
  • Account credentials and profile information associated with your role (e.g., volunteer, administrator, user)

2.2 Device Information

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system and version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers (where necessary for the operation of the App)

2.3 Location Data

If you enable location services, we may collect and process your device’s location data (e.g., latitude and longitude) to provide:

  • Navigation and safety features
  • Emergency response and SOS functionality
  • Location-based alerts and updates
  • Lost and found reporting with place-of-incident information

You can disable location access in your device settings; some features may not work without it.

2.4 Camera Access

With your permission, we may access your device’s camera to allow you to report incidents or upload photos (e.g., for lost person reports). Photos are used only for the purposes you authorize within the App.

2.5 Usage Data and Analytics

We may collect information about how you use the App, such as feature usage, session duration, and general interaction data, to improve performance and user experience.

2.6 Push Notification Tokens

To send you push notifications (e.g., emergency alerts, announcements, or important updates), we may collect and use notification tokens provided by your device or by Firebase Cloud Messaging.

2.7 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect financial or payment information (e.g., credit card numbers) within the App. Any in-app purchases or payments are processed by the Google Play Store under Google’s privacy policy. We do not knowingly collect sensitive categories of data (e.g., health, race, religion) except where you voluntarily provide such information in the context of the App’s safety or support features (e.g., emergency or lost-person reports), and we use it only for the stated purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect and process information in accordance with their own privacy policies:

3.1 Google Firebase

  • Firebase Authentication — for user sign-in and account management
  • Cloud Firestore — for storing app data (e.g., user profiles, reports, alerts)
  • Firebase Cloud Storage — for storing files such as images (e.g., lost person photos)

Google’s privacy policy applies to Firebase services: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.2 Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

We use FCM to deliver push notifications. FCM may collect device and usage information as described in Google’s privacy policy.

3.3 Google AdMob

We may display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and device information for ad delivery and measurement. For more information, see the Advertising section below and Google’s advertising privacy policy.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers to understand how they handle your information.

4. Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

  • AdMob may collect and use advertising IDs (e.g., Android Advertising ID), device information, and usage data to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • This data is processed in accordance with Google’s advertising policies and privacy practices.
  • You can learn more about how Google uses data for advertising and manage your ad preferences at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. App Permissions

The App may request the following permissions, and we use them only for the stated purposes:

  • Internet access — to communicate with our servers, Firebase, and third-party services and to load content and ads.
  • Camera — to capture photos for incident or lost person reports when you choose to use these features.
  • Location — to provide navigation, safety features, emergency response, and location-based alerts when you enable location services.
  • Notifications — to send you push notifications such as emergency alerts and important updates.
  • Storage — when required, to save or access files (e.g., images) on your device for app functionality.

You may revoke permissions in your device settings; doing so may limit or disable certain features.

6. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the App
  • Improve safety and emergency response during pilgrimage events
  • Provide user support and respond to your requests
  • Send important notifications, including emergency alerts and announcements
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Display advertisements through AdMob (as described above)
  • Comply with applicable laws and enforce our terms of use

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption of data in transit (e.g., HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and secure storage practices where applicable. Data stored with our service providers (e.g., Firebase) is protected in accordance with their security policies. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law — to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal process, or governmental requests
  • To provide app services — with trusted service providers (such as Google Firebase and AdMob) that assist us in operating the App, subject to contractual obligations to protect your data
  • To protect rights and safety — when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of users, the public, or ourselves

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed in regions where our service providers (including Google Firebase and Google AdMob) operate, which may be outside your country of residence. We take steps to ensure that such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions) where required by applicable data protection laws.

10. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA/UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data on the following bases: (1) Contract — to provide the App and its features; (2) Legitimate interests — to improve the App, ensure security, and communicate with you; (3) Consent — where we ask for your consent (e.g., optional analytics or marketing). You may withdraw consent at any time. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights: (1) Right to know — what personal information we collect, use, and disclose; (2) Right to delete — request deletion of your personal information (see the "Your Rights" section below for how to request account and data deletion); (3) Right to correct — request correction of inaccurate personal information; (4) Right to opt out of sale or sharing — we do not sell or "share" your personal information as defined under the CCPA; (5) Right to limit use of sensitive personal information — we use sensitive personal information only as permitted. We do not discriminate against you for exercising these rights. To exercise your rights, contact us using the details in the Contact Information section.

12. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at the email below, and we will take steps to delete it.

13. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the App’s services, fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or comply with legal obligations. When data is no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize it in accordance with our retention practices.

14. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and operational requirements
  • Object or restrict processing — where applicable by law

Request account and data deletion: You may request that your account and associated data be deleted at any time by using our dedicated form: Request account deletion. You can also send a deletion request by email to the address in the Contact Information section below.

To exercise any other rights or raise privacy concerns, please contact us using the details in the Contact Information section below.
